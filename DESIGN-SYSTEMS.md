## How to Use These

**In Claude Code, say any of these:**

```
"Read the Stripe design system and build me a pricing page in that style"

"I want my app to feel like Linear — read their design system and apply it"

"Build a landing page inspired by the Vercel design system"

"Mix the Spotify dark mode vibe with Apple's spacing and typography"
```

Claude reads the DESIGN.md file for that company and follows their exact design language — colors, spacing, typography, component patterns, everything.
